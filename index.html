About LINQ RESEARCH!

Serving humanity by improving the quality of medicine

LINQ Research LLC is a network of investigator sites collaborating with physicians and pharmaceutical sponsors in the greater Houston area. Our mission is to serve humanity by upholding the highest standards of medicine. Over the past decade, we have successfully completed numerous clinical trials. With our skilled research team, we prioritize a patient-centered approach in all our clinical trials.
