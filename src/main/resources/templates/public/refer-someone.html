Take the first step to a Brighter Future

At LINQ Research, we believe in the power of hope and the promise of a healthier future. We are actively seeking participants for our open enrollment trials and warmly invite you to join us on this transformative journey.

By joining a clinical trial, you pave the way for innovative treatments and cures by unlocking new possibilities in healthcare, bringing hope and healing to countless lives. Step forward and be the catalyst for a healthier future. Together, we can make a meaningful difference!

Participating in a clinical trial is at no cost and open to everyone, regardless of age or background. Participants may also receive compensation for their time and travel.

Interested in participating?

Visit our current studies page to find a trial that fits your needs and see if you qualify for any of our ongoing research studies. Once you apply, a Patient Enrollment Specialist will contact you. They will guide you through the study details and answer any questions you may have throughout the process.
