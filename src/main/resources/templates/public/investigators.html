Murtaza Mussaji, DO

Dr. Murtaza Mussaji, a board-certified internal medicine physician, has been practicing in Houston since 2007. He earned his medical degree from Midwestern University in Arizona and completed his residency at UTHealth inHouston. Affiliated with Memorial Hermann Hospital.

Dr. Mussaji's clinical interests include diabetes, geriatrics, hypertension, asthma, COPD, heart disease, thyroid disorders, obesity, depression, and anxiety. He speaks Spanish, Hindi, Gujarati, and Swahili. He provides comprehensive, personalized care for patients of all ages, blending family medicine, internal medicine, and urgent care.
