Meet the Founders

Together Hussein and Mustafa have dedicated over 15 years to the field of clinical research, striving to deliver inclusive and high-quality clinical research to the Houston area.

In 2014, Hussein Aladin and Mustafa Noorbhai founded LINQ Research LLC with a shared vision in mind: to shape the future of healthcare through a supportive, informative, and data-driven clinical research environment.

Hussein Aladin

CEO/ Co-Founder

Hussein Aladin is a clinical research professional, with over 15 years of extensive experience. He graduated Cum Laude from the University of Houston, earning a degree in International Business and Communications. Hussein is a proud member of the Association of Clinical Research Professionals (ACRP) and holds the ACRP-CP certification. Throughout his career, Hussein has worked at various levels within the clinical research industry, including sponsor, Contract Research Organization (CRO), institution, and site management organization. His diverse background has equipped him with a comprehensive understanding of the clinical research process and a commitment to enhancing healthcare outcomes. Hussein's passion for research is evident in his dedication to his work and his continuous pursuit of excellence.

Outside of his professional life, he enjoys hiking, exploring different restaurants, and spending quality time with his wife and son.
